Zhe Cao

I am a research scientist at Facebook Reality Labs (FRL). My research focuses on perceiving and tracking 3D humans to enable immersive VR/AR experience. Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. from UC Berkeley advised by Prof. Jitendra Malik. Before that, I graduated from CMU Robotics Institute advised by Prof. Yaser Sheikh.
